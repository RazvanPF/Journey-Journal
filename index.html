Welcome to Journey App!

Journey App is your personal digital journal designed to help you log and manage your life experiences with ease. You can personalize entries with text formatting and images. Each entry allows you to document your mood, weather, location, and date, providing a comprehensive snapshot of your day. The app supports saving your entries, so your memories are preserved just the way you left them. Whether you want to reflect on your past or plan your future, Journey App is the perfect companion to document your life's journey.

Instructions:

  • Adds a new journal entry you can edit.
  • Toggles remove mode. Click on any entry with it toggled on to remove the entry.
  • Saves current entry list so you can find everything as you left it upon return.
  • Clears all entries and all save data so you can start from scratch.
  • Note: By disabling content editing inside the entry, you can the added URLs by clicking on them.
